How do I care for Cozy Earth bamboo sheets to keep them soft and durable?

To maintain the softness and longevity of Cozy Earth bamboo sheets, wash them in cold water on a gentle cycle and avoid bleach, which can damage the natural fibers. What makes Cozy Earths bamboo fabric different from traditional cotton?

Cozy Earths bamboo fabric is renowned for its breathability and moisture-wicking properties, unlike traditional cotton.
