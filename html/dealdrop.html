About Minky Couture

Minky Couture offers luxurious, high-quality blankets designed to provide ultimate comfort and style. Founded with a passion for creating the softest blankets possible, each Minky Couture blanket is crafted with meticulous attention to detail and made from plush, premium materials. The company provides a wide variety of colors, patterns, and sizes to suit every taste and occasion, making them perfect for snuggling, gift-giving, or adding an elegant touch to home decor. Minky Couture Return Policy

The Minky Couture allows customers to return items within 45 days of order delivery, provided they are unused and unwashed. Custom orders and final sale items are non-returnable. A flat rate shipping fee of $10 is applied to refunds, which are processed to the original payment method once items are received. Exchanges are available, with in-store exchanges being issued on a merchandise gift card. Seasonal items have a specific return window. For details on the full policy, customers can refer to the return page.
